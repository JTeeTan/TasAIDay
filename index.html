Tasmania Government AI Discovery Day

Across the Australian Public Service, AI is rapidly becoming a core capability for delivering better services, improving decision making and strengthening trust with citizens.

Through practical insights, real use cases and proven patterns, this session examines how governance, platforms and operating models enable agencies to deliver AI securely and at scale.
