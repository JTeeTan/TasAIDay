Tasmania Government AI Discovery Day

Across the Australian Public Service, AI is rapidly becoming a core capability for delivering better services, improving decision making and strengthening trust with citizens. The opportunity is no longer just about exploring what AI could do, but about the pathway to deploying it responsibly, securely and repeatably.

Through practical insights, real use cases and proven patterns, we’ll examine how governance, platforms and operating models can unlock progress, enabling agencies to deliver secure, auditable and repeatable AI at scale.
